About


Little Kingdom Nursery School run by Railway Women Welfare Central Organisation (RWWCO), is just like a home away from home. We emphasize on the overall development and on the self confidence building of the child. We have a good quality study material. Besides all this, we celebrate all the festivals with great enthusiasm and joy. We teach the kids to respect the global culture. We teach them moral values and social etiquette.
